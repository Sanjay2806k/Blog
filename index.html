MS DHONI 


MS Dhoni is a retired Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is known for his exceptional wicket-keeping skills, calm captaincy, and finishing abilities in limited-overs cricket.

VIRAT KOHLI



Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is considered one of the best batsmen in the world, known for his aggressive and consistent performances.


ROHIT SHARMA

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer known for his explosive batting skills and holds several records in limited-overs cricket. He has won numerous awards and is the vice-captain of the Indian cricket team.
